Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison Posts Strong Earnings in FY2021, Reaching the Culmination of Its Turnaround Strategy

Share

Font Size

  • A
  • A
  • Total Revenue increased by 12.4% YoY to IDR31,388.3 billion.
  • EBITDA increased by 21.4% YoY to IDR13,885.5 billion. EBITDA margin stood at 44.2% in FY 2021.
  • Net Profit for The Year attributable to Owners of The Parents is recorded at IDR6,750.9 billion.
  • The Company’s customer base increased 4.4% to 62.9 million in FY 2021 compared to the previous year.
  • Average Revenue per User (ARPU) increased to IDR34.4 thousand, from previously IDR31.9 thousand in FY 2020.
